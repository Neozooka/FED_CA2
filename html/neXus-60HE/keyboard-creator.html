About the Creator: Deng Jing Heng

The neXus 60HE exists because Deng Jing Heng refused to lose another match to input lag.

An obsessive competitive gamer and engineer, Jing Heng grew tired of flimsy retail keyboards and sluggish switches. Driven by the Built For Gamers, By Gamers ethos, he engineered a zero-compromise solution from the ground up.

He pioneered the 60HE’s ultra-responsive magnetic Hall Effect actuation, sourced high-impact premium polymers to eliminate chassis flex, and tuned the hardware to sync flawlessly with neXus OS. He designs for the ultimate competitive edge, enforcing the exact standards he demands for his own setup.
